### Release Goal:
Our goal for this release is to deliver a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for CashTag, which is a smart expense-tracking web application. The MVP will allow users to track personal and group expenses, set and monitor their monthly budgets, and visualize their spending patterns through various graphs and end-of-the-month reports.

### Scope of the Release (Epics & Key Features):
1. User Authentication (Sign up and login functionality)
2. Expense Tracking (Add, view, and categorize expenses)
3. Analytics & Visualization (View monthly spending trends through charts and reports)
4. Group Expense Management (Create and join groups, split shared expenses)
5. Budget Management (Create monthly budgets and track spending)  
6. Gamification & Notifications/Reminders (Earn badges for positive reinforcement and view budget limit notifications and other reminders to stay on track)
